Cutting Hair since 1999

Established in 1999, The Hairdresser is the go-to barber shop in London City. Specialising in male grooming, we offer everything from trims and tidy-ups to re-styles and wet shaves. Our highly skilled staff can advise on the latest trends and recommend cuts to suit specific hair types. We cannot guarantee a walk-in appointment so call in advance to book. We are open six days a week and until 8pm on Thursdays.
